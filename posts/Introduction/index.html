Background

I am a recent college graduate from the Barrett Honors College at Arizona State University (ASU) with a Bachelors of Science in Mathematics who is very interested in data science, tool development and cybersecurity. During my time at ASU, I worked on several different research projects mostly focused on computer science and software development, but also including work in both wet labs and cybersecurity. Although my degree is in mathematics, I spent my time focusing on applying it to computer engineering and cryptography. I have also taken most of the core courses for computer science students including algorithms and all of the base language courses. I am proficient in numerous coding languages including Python [2.7, 3.5, 3.6], C/C++, Java, JavaScript (JS), Structured Query Language (SQL)[MySQL, Sqlite, postgresql], HTML, CSS, PHP, R, Bash, Windows PowerShell, Assembly (x86), Clingo, Cplus2ASP, Wolfram Mathematica, and Matlab. And have experience with common data science tools, common security tools, popular Python, Java, C, and R packages/libraries, and database management. Additionally, I have experience with agile and the software development lifecycle and code base management software including GitLab and GitHub. I also have hardware familiarity with desktops, laptops, servers, Raspberry Pi, routers and modems (Office and Home networking). Finally, I have administrator level experience with Linux: Debian, Ubuntu (including LTS) Arch, Kali, Buscador, Black Box, Parrot Security, Windows: Home, Professional, and Server, and Mac OS.
